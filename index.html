About Us

Velantraa Exports LLP was founded by three visionary women entrepreneurs with a shared passion for bringing India's rich natural and agricultural heritage to the global marketplace. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful belief: that India's diverse natural resources, cultivated through generations of traditional wisdom, deserve recognition and appreciation worldwide.

As a women-led enterprise, we bring a unique perspective to the export industry, combining attention to detail, ethical business practices and a deep understanding of both local producers and international market needs. Our team works tirelessly to ensure that every product leaving our shores meets the highest standards of quality, sustainability and compliance with global regulations.

At Velantraa Exports LLP, we pride ourselves on our professional approach, transparent operations and commitment to building lasting relationships with our partners across the globe. Our expertise in navigating complex international trade regulations, coupled with our extensive network of trusted suppliers, enables us to deliver excellence in every shipment.

From sourcing the finest agricultural products to ensuring they reach international markets in perfect condition, we manage every aspect of the export process with precision and care. Our dedication to quality, reliability and customer satisfaction has established us as a trusted name in the export industry.
